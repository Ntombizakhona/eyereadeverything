# Milk and honey
**by** 
*Rupi Kaur*

This was an interesting collection of “poems”.
Might be more appealing to an adolescent, but if you have experienced “love” and “heartbreak”. 
These poems are a bit, uh, interesting, I suppose.
A quick read.
